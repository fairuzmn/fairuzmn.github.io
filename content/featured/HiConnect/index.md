hiconnect is a secure mobile application by [PT Bank Hibank Indonesia](https://www.hibank.co.id/), developed to support the bank’s Cash Management System (CMS) for corporate clients. The app enables companies to authorize and manage transactions through a soft token scan system, ensuring secure, real-time access to financial operations. As part of hibank’s digital ecosystem, hiconnect enhances corporate banking by streamlining approvals and strengthening transaction security.
